Japanese Festival

Chris Williams, Kayla Carr, Megan Elrod, Patrick Noble

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

Description

Explore the world of a Japanese festival, filled with minigames, decorative scenes, and cute squishy people! Try your hand at smashing watermelons, shooting targets, and dancing to your favorite songs to earn points. Then use those points to customize your character and buy more songs.

Controls

  • WASD = Move around
  • Enter = Approach a booth and check out it's contents
  • Move Mouse = Observe the world from a 3rd person perspective Each minigame has different controls which will be displayed as you start playing it

Libraries

  • FreeType2 for text on-screen
  • OpenCV for playing videos
  • irrKlang for playing audio
  • GLFW for window and time management
  • GLEW for OpenGL extension management

Technologies Implemented

  • 3rd person controllable camera
  • Complex environment with rigged animation
  • Collision detection by XZ radius check
  • Planar shadows
  • Audio playback
  • Video playback
  • HUD with billboarded text on-screen
  • TODO View frustum culling
  • TODO Cook-Torrance shader
